# Why Telegram Mini Apps

Eat2Earn is built inside **Telegram Mini Apps** to ensure:

* No app downloads
* Low device requirements
* Global reach
* Native social sharing

Telegram acts as:

* Identity layer
* Distribution layer
* Engagement layer

This allows Eat2Earn to scale quickly while remaining accessible.

***

### What This Documentation Covers

This GitBook explains:

* How Eat2Earn works
* How rewards are calculated

* How the platform evolves over time

The goal is full transparency and clarity.

### Important Disclaimer

Eat2Earn rewards participation and activity.\
It does not guarantee financial returns.

Any future token utilities are designed to support the ecosystem and community participation, not speculation.

Eat2Earn does not ask users to change how they eat.\
