# Eat to Earn ($EATT) Whitepaper

## 1. Introduction

Eat to Earn is a Web3 rewards platform built as a Telegram Mini App.  
Its goal is to turn everyday food purchases into on-chain rewards.

Instead of changing user behavior, Eat to Earn rewards what people already do: eat.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Millions of people eat outside or order food daily, yet:
- Loyalty programs are fragmented
- Rewards are locked inside centralized platforms
- Users receive little long-term value

At the same time, Web3 adoption struggles due to:
- Complex onboarding
- Wallet friction
- App downloads

---

## 3. Solution

Eat to Earn solves both problems by combining:
- A Telegram Mini App (no app download)
- Simple bill scanning
- A gamified rewards system
- Crypto-native incentives

Users simply:
1. Eat at any restaurant  
2. Scan their bill inside Telegram  
3. Earn points and $EATT rewards  

---

## 4. Product Overview

### 4.1 Telegram Mini App
Eat to Earn operates entirely inside Telegram:
- No additional app installs
- Familiar UX
- Easy onboarding

### 4.2 Scan & Verify
Bills are scanned only to verify purchases.
No personal or sensitive data is stored beyond verification needs.

### 4.3 Points System
Users earn points through:
- Bill scans
- Social tasks
- Early participation

Points increase eligibility for future rewards and airdrops.

---

## 5. Gamified Elements (Non-Game)

Eat to Earn is **not a game**, but includes light gamification:
- XP progression
- Levels
- Badges
- Seasonal challenges

These elements increase engagement without complexity.

---

## 6. Token Utility ($EATT)

$EATT is the native reward token of the ecosystem.

Utility includes:
- Reward distribution
- Airdrops to early users
- Future ecosystem incentives
- Community participation

$EATT is designed as a utility and reward token, not a speculative instrument.

---

## 7. Tokenomics Overview

Token distribution is designed to prioritize users.

Indicative allocation:
- Community Rewards & Airdrops: 40%
- Early User Airdrops: 10%
- Ecosystem & Growth: 20%
- Liquidity: 20%
- Team & Operations: 10%

Exact details may evolve as the ecosystem grows.

May be eligible for future $EATT airdrops based on points and activity.
